# Contributing to Todoclaw

Thanks for your interest! A few notes before you open a pull request.

## License & CLA

Todoclaw is licensed under the **GNU AGPL-3.0** (see `LICENSE`).

Before any external contribution can be merged, contributors must agree to the
project's **Contributor License Agreement** (`CLA.md`). This lets the maintainer
keep the project's licensing options open (including the dual-licensing
reservation in `COPYRIGHT`) while you keep ownership of your own work.

> Automated CLA sign-off (via [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io)) is not
> wired up yet — it will be enabled if and when the project starts accepting
> outside contributions. Until then, agreement can be recorded by a comment on
> the pull request stating that you have read and agree to `CLA.md`.

## Workflow

See `docs/COLLABORATION.md` for branch naming, PR conventions, and CI
expectations.
